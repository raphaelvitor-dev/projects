Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the typical turnaround time to set everything up?
    Generally, it takes about 2-4 weeks. This timeframe includes the initial setup (such as Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or Google Local Service Ads), design, development, website creation, and testing phases.
  • Can I request additional modifications to my website after it's built?
    Yes, we offer unlimited modifications to your website within the plan, ensuring your site stays updated without any additional costs.
  • What is the purpose of Google Ads and how does it benefit my business?
    Google Ads allows you to create targeted ads that appear in Google search results and across the Google Display Network. This helps drive relevant traffic to your website and increases your brand’s visibility. By targeting specific keywords and demographics, you can attract potential customers who are actively searching for products or services like yours.
  • How can Facebook Ads help in reaching my target audience?
    Facebook Ads enable you to target users based on their interests, behaviors, demographics, and location. This precision helps you connect with your ideal audience and increase engagement. With various ad formats and targeting options, Facebook Ads can effectively drive traffic, generate leads, and boost brand awareness.
  • What are Google Local Service Ads and how do they differ from regular Google Ads?
    Google Local Service Ads operate on a pay-per-lead basis, meaning you pay for each qualified lead. These ads are designed for businesses serving local areas and appear at the top of Google search results with a Google Guarantee badge, which helps build trust with potential customers. Unlike regular Google Ads, which target keywords broadly, Local Service Ads focus on local searches and provide a direct way for customers to contact you via phone or message. If a lead is not qualified, you can contest the charge and potentially receive a refund from Google (up to $2,000).
